ValueSet: FR Core ValueSet Contact relationship

Official URL: http://hl7.fr/ig/fhir/core/ValueSet/fr-core-contact-relationship Version: 2.0.0
Standards status: Informative Maturity Level: 1 Computable Name: FRCoreValueSetContactRelationship
